Your Pet's Home away from Home!

Welcome to Happy Tails Pet Hotel and Spa! Alongside our per diem rates, we offer several Group-Day Deals to suit your length of stay. Each Group-Day Deal comes with a standard grooming package and kennel bed. If you'd like to spoil your pup, you can upgrade to a luxury suite or add a spa treatment to your package for an additional fee.

Devoted play time twice a day

Healthy pets are happy pets, so we make sure to devote at least a cumulative hour every day to hands-on, active play. This often involves extra social interaction with the other guests, but we can arrange one-on-one sessions if desired.

Grooming services are included in our Group-Day Deals.

We provide a standard wash and trim at no extra charge with our Group-Day Deals. If you're looking for a little extra loving for your furry friend, our spa treatment add-on packages start as low as $8.99.
